Our Story

Dressing India, One Outfit at a Time

TrendyMart was born from a simple belief: great style shouldn't cost a fortune. Founded in 2019 by a team of fashion enthusiasts in Mumbai, we started as a small boutique with 50 products and big dreams.

Today, we serve over 16,000 happy customers across India, offering more than 500 curated products that blend international trends with Indian sensibilities. Every piece in our collection is handpicked by our team of style experts.

How We Grew

Our Journey

2019
The Spark
TrendyMart launched in Mumbai with 50 curated products and a small team of 5 passionate people. Our first month brought 200 orders.
2020
Going Digital
We launched our full e-commerce platform during the pandemic, making fashion accessible across India without stepping out.
2022
10,000 Customers Milestone
We hit 10,000 happy customers, expanded our team to 28 people, and launched our mobile app for a seamless shopping experience.
2024
Sustainability Pledge
Launched our 'Green Thread' initiative — committing 20% of our catalogue to sustainable and ethically sourced products.
2025
Today & Beyond
16,000+ customers, 500+ products, and expanding to our first physical store in Mumbai. The journey continues!
